CrystalCollector!

How to play?

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal below. By clicking on q crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your totatl score.

You win the game by making your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the randome number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
